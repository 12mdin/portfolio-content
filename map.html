A Map of My Island

My island is named Buji Island, and there are 10 villagers live on the island. Here is a map showing some important locations on the island - villagers' home, resident service, nook's cranny, able sisters, museum, and airport.

On the island, you can catching insects, diving for sea creatures, and fishing for fish. You can find different critters depending on the season and time of the day. Each newly discovered creature is automatically added to the in-game critterpedia.

For each new creature you collected, you can donating them to the museum. Blathers will accept new species of bugs and fish and analyze any fossils you dig up.

Even more, you can have some coffee and the Roost cafe, which is located inside of the museum. use the amiibo phone inside the cafe to invite other characteds to join you by using compatible amiibo cards and figures.

If you click on the avatars on the left or the buildings on the right, you will be directed to an external website for more information (only works on tablet/desktop view)!
